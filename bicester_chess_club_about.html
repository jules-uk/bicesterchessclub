About Us

Bicester Chess Club is a community of chess enthusiasts who meet regularly to play and improve their game. Our club welcomes players of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced players. We host weekly meetups, monthly tournaments, and special events throughout the year.

Our mission is to promote the game of chess in the Bicester area and provide a friendly and supportive environment for players to learn and enjoy the game. Whether you are looking to compete or just play for fun, Bicester Chess Club has something for everyone.

Join us and be part of a vibrant chess community!
